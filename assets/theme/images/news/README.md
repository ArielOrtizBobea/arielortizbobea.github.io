# News article images

Drop article hero images here and reference them from `_data/news.yml` as:

    image: /assets/theme/images/news/your_filename.jpg

Recommended format:
  - JPEG or PNG
  - Landscape orientation (16:10 roughly); images are cropped to 16:10 on the
    home page and rendered at 200px wide on the /news/ page
  - 800px wide is plenty; no need for huge files

Naming convention suggestion: outlet_YYYYMM_shortslug.jpg
  e.g.  nyt_202604_ag_climate.jpg
        bloomberg_202603_emissions.jpg
